Model & Input Details 📊

The prediction system considers multiple real-world factors to estimate food delivery time accurately.

Distance 📍

The total distance between restaurant and delivery location directly impacts delivery time.

Traffic 🚦

Traffic conditions (Low, Medium, High) significantly affect delivery speed and time.

Weather 🌦️

Weather conditions like rainy, foggy, or clear influence delivery delays and travel safety.

Preparation Time ⏱️

Time taken by the restaurant to prepare the order is a key factor in total delivery time.

Courier Experience 👨‍💼

Experienced delivery agents tend to deliver faster and more efficiently.

Vehicle Type 🛵

Different vehicles like bike, scooter, or car affect delivery speed.

Time of Day ☀️

Delivery time varies based on morning, afternoon, evening, or night.

ML Model 🤖

Random Forest Regression model is used to predict delivery time with high accuracy.
